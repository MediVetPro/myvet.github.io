Refund Policy

Thank you for using our services. Below is our refund policy for auto-renewing subscriptions made through Google Play.

1. Service Description

We offer various auto-renewing subscription plans through Google Play. By subscribing to one of our plans, you agree to recurring payments according to the billing cycle of the selected plan.

2. Refund Policy

Auto-renewing subscriptions are non-refundable once payment has been processed. However, you may cancel your subscription at any time before the next renewal date to avoid future charges. Cancellation can be done from the subscriptions page in Google Play.

To request a refund during the free trial period (if available), you must cancel the subscription before the end of the trial period. Refunds outside the trial period are not guaranteed and are subject to Google Play's refund policy.

3. Subscription Cancellation

To cancel your subscription, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your Android device.
  2. Go to the "Subscriptions" section in your account menu.
  3. Select the subscription you want to cancel.
  4. Tap "Cancel subscription" and follow the instructions.

For more details, please refer to the Google Play Refund Policy.
